How it works

Creators set their own goals and milestones with the smart contract

Backers fund creative projects and get updates as milestones are met

Backers receive PERK tokens for each project they support and use these for rewards or trade them with other backers for different perks.

Failed projects are partially refunded with the RYO. Backers can use RYO to contribute to other projects.

Successful creators are able to build and grow their business on Hanzo even after their crowdfunding campaign is over, so the community they have built stays with them as they grow their company.
